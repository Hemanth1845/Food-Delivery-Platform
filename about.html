Our Story

FoodExpress was founded in 2015 with a simple mission: to connect people with their favorite local restaurants and deliver delicious food right to their doorstep.

What started as a small operation in a single neighborhood has now grown into a platform serving thousands of customers across multiple cities. Our journey has been fueled by our passion for food and commitment to exceptional service.

We believe that good food has the power to bring people together, and we're proud to be a part of countless meals, celebrations, and everyday moments in our customers' lives.

Our Mission

At FoodExpress, our mission is to make food delivery accessible, affordable, and enjoyable for everyone. We strive to:

  • Connect customers with the best local restaurants
  • Ensure fast and reliable delivery service
  • Support local businesses and communities
  • Provide exceptional customer service
  • Innovate and improve the food delivery experience
