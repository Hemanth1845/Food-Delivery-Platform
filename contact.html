Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can place an order through our website or mobile app. Simply browse the menu, select your items, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to create an account or log in to complete your order.

What are your delivery hours?

We deliver during our operating hours, which are 10am-10pm Monday through Saturday, and 11am-9pm on Sundays. Delivery times may vary depending on your location and the restaurant's preparation time.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is confirmed, you can track its status in real-time through our website or app. You'll receive notifications when your order is being prepared, when it's out for delivery, and when it's about to arrive.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and cash on delivery. All online payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

How can I become a delivery partner?

If you're interested in becoming a delivery partner, please fill out the application form on our website or contact our recruitment team at careers@foodexpress.com. We're always looking for reliable and enthusiastic individuals to join our team!
